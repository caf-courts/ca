Investigations Office

About the Office

The Broadview County's Investigation Office is a task force comprised of detectives from various law enforcement agencies. Its primary function is to coordinate and collaborate on complex or high-profile investigations that require the expertise and resources of multiple agencies. Officers within the County Investigations Office are retained on a part-time basis.

 The detectives within the task force work in tandem to share information, coordinate efforts, and leverage their respective agency's capabilities to solve crimes and apprehend perpetrators. They primarily focus on cases that demand specialized expertise or extensive investigation, such as organized crime.

 The County Investigations Office, from time to time, may receive warrants before other departments do if it is a matter of public significance.
