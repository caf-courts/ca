About the Court

The Appellate Division of the Broadview County Court is the highest tribunal in the County for all cases and controversies arising under the Constitution, Character or the laws of the United States and Broadview County. As the final arbiter of the law, the Court is charged with ensuring the people of Broadview County the promise of equal justice under law and, thereby, also functions as guardian and interpreter of the Constitution and County Charter.

The Court consists of the Chief Judge and four Associate Judges. Power to nominate the Judges is vested in the County Executive of the Broadview County, and appointments are made with the advice and ratification of the County Comission.
